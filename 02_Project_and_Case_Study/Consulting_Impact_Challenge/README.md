## üéØ Problem Statement

WPE is a technically strong engineering organisation but faces structural challenges:

- Heavy dependency on a small group of defence clients  
- Minimal business development (BD) capabilities  
- Weak digital presence and no marketing function  
- Undocumented internal processes  
- Limited risk governance  
- A reactive commercial approach rather than strategic pipeline building  
- Need to scale Poland operations and diversify sectors  

The company must evolve from engineering-led survival to a market-driven **Manufacturing-as-a-Service (MaaS)** provider.
